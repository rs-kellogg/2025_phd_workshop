On June 25, 2025, Mr. Alex Chen resigned as Chief Financial Officer of Orion Corp., effective immediately. In connection with his 
resignation and pursuant to the terms of his pre-existing severance agreement, Mr. Chen will receive a lump-sum payment of $3,200,000, 
along with accelerated vesting of all outstanding equity awards, as full and final settlement of all claims. Orion Corp. acknowledges 
his contributions during his tenure.
