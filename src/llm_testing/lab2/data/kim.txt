Effective August 15, 2025, Mr. David Kim concluded his tenure as Chief Technology Officer of Global Solutions Corp. by mutual agreement. 
In addition to a cash severance payment of $1,800,000, Mr. Kim's severance package includes several non-monetary benefits: Global Solutions 
Corp. will provide executive outplacement services for a period of twelve (12) months, continuation of health and dental benefits for 
twenty-four (24) months at company expense, and, due to his high-profile role and recent threats, professional personal security (bodyguard) 
services for a period of six (6) months post-departure.
