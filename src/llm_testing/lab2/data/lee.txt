On July 1, 2025, Ms. Brenda Lee's employment as Chief Operating Officer of Apex Innovations Inc. was terminated without cause. Pursuant 
to her executive severance plan, Ms. Lee will receive a severance payment equal to eighteen (18) months of her current annual base salary of 
$400,000. Additionally, she will receive a pro-rated portion of her target annual bonus for the fiscal year 2025, calculated based on her 
target bonus of 50 percent of her base salary for the six months she was employed in the current fiscal year. These payments will be disbursed
 in equal monthly installments over the next 18 months.
